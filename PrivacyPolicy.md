# Privacy Policy

**Sleep Reflection Journal**  
Last Updated: February 2026

---

## Overview

Sleep Reflection Journal ("the App") is designed with your privacy as a fundamental priority. This policy explains how we handle your information.

---

## Data Collection

### What We Collect
The App collects only the information you explicitly provide:
- Your reflection responses (mental state, body state, sleep quality, etc.)
- Your preferred check-in times
- Your notification preferences

### What We Do NOT Collect
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Health data from HealthKit
- Contacts or photos

---

## Data Storage

**All data is stored locally on your device.**

- Data is saved using iOS secure storage (UserDefaults)
- Data is encrypted by iOS standard device encryption
- Data never leaves your device
- Data is not synced to any cloud service
- Data is not accessible to us or any third party

---

## Data Sharing

**We do not share your data with anyone.**

- No data is transmitted to our servers
- No data is shared with third parties
- No advertising networks receive your data
- No analytics services track your usage

---

## Data Retention

- Data remains on your device until you delete it
- You can delete individual reflections from History
- You can delete all data from Settings → Data & Privacy
- Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all data

---

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:
- **Access**: View all your data within the App
- **Export**: Download your data as a text file
- **Delete**: Remove individual or all reflections
- **Portability**: Export and save to your Files app

---

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App contains no in-app purchases, advertising, or social features.
